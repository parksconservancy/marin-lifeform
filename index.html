Marin County Lifeform Map - 2019

The Marin County lifeform map is a 26-class land use and land cover map of Marin County, reflecting the state of the landscape in summer, 2018. This product is a simplification of the fine scale vegetation map, with a fraction of the total map classes. The fine scale vegetation map has significantly more floristic detail than the lifeform map; woody vegetation communities in the 82-class fine scale map are mapped to the alliance level of the National Vegetation Classification.
